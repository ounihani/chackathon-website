What is Chackathon?

CHACKATHON is our version of the famous Hackathon event.



Through this event, we will gather Tunisian skills from several universities, for a 12-hour competition of Coding. During which, they will be interested in solving 16 algorithmic problems made available one after the other.



Indeed, our event is out of the ordinary, we aim to gather 150 people between participants and visitors. The challenge will be to manage stress, time and solve as many problems as possible.
